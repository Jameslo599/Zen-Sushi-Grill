Frequently Asked Questions

  • Are there vegetarian options?

    Yes! We provide a variety of vegetarian choices on our menu and can further customize existing dishes to fit your dietary choices.

  • Do you have gluten-free options?

    Absolutely! We can make almost any dish on our menu gluten-free (excluding tempura), and we will notify you of any sauces that contain gluten.

  • Is this an AYCE (All-you-can-eat) restaurant?

    We do not provide AYCE.

  • Are you BYOB?

    Since we stock a full bar, we are do not allow outside alcoholic beverages.

  • Are you associated with other restaurants?

    We are completely independent and are not associated with any other restaurant.
